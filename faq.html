Understanding ticket policies

Ticket refund and exchange policies vary by theatre and by the terms of your purchase. Online ticket platforms may have different rules than in-person sales. Typically, tickets are non-refundable once purchased, but some venues allow exchanges for a different showtime on the same day, subject to availability and potential fees. If you arrive late and miss a significant portion of the film, re-entry or refund policies are at the discretion of theatre management.

When purchasing tickets online, review the terms and conditions before completing checkout. Look for information on cancellation deadlines, service fees, and whether your confirmation is transferable. If you have questions about a specific purchase, contact the box office or the online ticketing provider's customer service before your showtime.
