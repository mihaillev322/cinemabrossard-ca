Tickets, pricing signals, and purchase flow

Purchasing movie tickets brossard can be done online or in person at the box office. Each method has its own workflow and considerations. Online purchases typically require selecting a film, choosing a showtime, picking seats (if seat selection is available), and completing payment with a credit or debit card. After checkout, you may receive a confirmation email or QR code to present at the theatre. In-person purchases allow you to ask staff questions and pay with cash or card, but may involve waiting in line during peak times.

What information you will need

Regardless of purchase method, be prepared to provide or confirm:

  • The film title and showtime
  • The number of tickets and any applicable discounts (senior, student, child)
  • Your preferred payment method
  • Contact information if booking online (email or phone number)
Online checkout checklist

When buying tickets online, follow these steps: select your film and showtime, choose the number of tickets, pick seats if the system allows, review the total cost including any fees, enter payment details, and save your confirmation email or QR code. Arrive at the theatre with your confirmation ready to scan or present at the box office or kiosk.

At-the-counter tips

If purchasing tickets in person, arrive with enough time before the showtime to avoid missing previews. Have your payment method ready, and ask staff about any current promotions or membership programs. If you have questions about accessibility, seating, or format, the box office is the best place to get immediate answers.

Refund and exchange notes

Refund and exchange policies vary by theatre and purchase method. Some venues allow exchanges up to a certain time before the showtime, while others may have no-refund policies for online purchases.
